PDP

The PDP (Power Distribution Board) is the panel which connects to the battery and distributes the power properly to all the motors, and all electrical systems on the robot.

RoboRio

The RoboRio is the brains of the robot, which is the primary connection to the FMS (Field Management System), as well as connecting to the drivestation computer.

RSL

The RSL is the robot status light, and it indicates the state of the robot. Blinking means enables, Solid means disabled, and blank means the robot is off.

Router

The router is what connects to the FMS and drivestation computer throuhg the RoboRio, so teams can connect throught ethernet or WiFi.

Limelight/Vision

Limelight and vision are used to see targets on the field, and then the robot can position itself or do an action accordingly. The light bouncess off of the retroreflective tape, and the camera on the bot can recognize this.

Coding

The robot can be coded using either C++ or Java, and Team 2702 uses C++ due to the ease of use, versitility, and functionality.
