FMS

The FMS is the Field Managment System, responsible for coordinating all the robots and connecting them via radio to the driver stations. It is composed of many electronics and a status light above the driver station.

Game

Every year there is a different game and goals for the robot to accomplish. This year (2020), there are yellow dodgeballs that can be shot into ports to score points, and teams can climb to earn more points.

Competition

The game is played with 3 robots against another 3 robots, and they work together to score the most points possible. Teams then are ranked and choose their alliance that that would like to pick based on their ranking, and a playoff round follows to determine the winner.

Judging

In the pits where robots are fixed and worked on, the judges come around and talk to teams about robot design and structure, and awards are given based on structural integrity, and team outreach.

Notable Teams

There are many great Canadian teams competiting at these events, and many of them advance to the world championships in Detroit. These include OP Robotics (2056), Simbotics (1114), and the Rebels (2702). One of the best teams is Orbit (1690), whose robot is shown below.
