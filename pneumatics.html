Air Tanks

The air tanks are usually round black cylinders, designed to store compressed air at 60 or 120 PSI (Pounds per Square Inch).

Compressor

The compresser takes air from the atmosphere and makes it into a more condensed state, and then puts it in the air tanks. This is later used to power mechanisms.

Pneunatic Cylinder

The pneumatic cylinder is an actuater that will push and pull parts on the robot using air pressure from the tanks. It is usually used to extend and retract mechanisms.

Solenoid

A solenoid is a pneumatic component which activates the cylinders via a button or an electrical signal. It is the main operator of the system.

Pancake

A pancake is another version of a pneumatic cylinder, and usually has a much smaller range, most of the time less than one inch. It is usually used for small mechanisms or for switching gears on the divetrain.
